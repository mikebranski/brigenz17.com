Couple

Dani and Mike are like two peas in a pod who met on Tinder. They love to laugh, binge watch Netflix shows, eat delicious food and put off doing the dishes together. Dani and Mike look forward to tying the knot in August and hope all of you wonderful people can be there to witness this event.

Mike Branski

Groom

Mike is a whacky guy with a big heart and an even bigger smile. He loves DIY and nerding out about, well, nerd things. He thrives on learning and strives toward to stoic and Eastern philosophy. From Star Wars and board games to fountain pens and space, his passion for life is palpable. Heck, his ex-girlfriend even once told him his "optimism can be a little offputting".

Dani Strigenz

Bride

Dani is a kind, fun-loving gal who is always willing to help people. She also is quite hilarious and always enjoys to add sarcasm to a conversation. Dani adores working with kids and is certain she has found her passion in life. When not at school, she loves a fantastic meal, vacation or movie.

Story

  • Nov 2014

    Dani and Mike had their first date on November 14, 2014. It started out by Dani getting lost on the way to Mike's condo (a typical situation that still happens to this day). On this historic evening, Mike and Dani went to Rock Bottom for dinner, had a cup of coffee at Collectivo, laughed their butts off at Comedy Sports and finally watched Home Alone at Mike's place. They also both knew that this was the beginning of something special.

  • Dec 2014

    Mike finally got the courage to officially ask Dani to be his girlfriend on December 13, 2014. What do you think she said?!?!

  • Dec 2015

    Mike and Dani bought their first home together in Wauwatosa. It was the second house that they looked at and they both immediately knew it was “the one”.

  • Aug 2016

    Mike finally blustered up the courage to ask Dani to marry her. I am not positive of her response, but I think she might have said yes.
